Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about deploying your AI employee.

Will the AI sound like a robot?

No. We use next-generation voice synthesis models that include breathing pauses, empathetic tones, and natural conversational flow. 90% of customers don't even realize they are speaking to an AI.

Can it handle multiple calls at the same time?

Yes! Whether you get 1 call or 10,000 calls at the exact same second, AI TaskOrbit handles all of them concurrently without putting anyone on hold.

How does it book appointments?

The AI integrates directly with your calendar (Calendly, CRM). It checks real-time availability and books the slot during the live call, instantly sending a confirmation WhatsApp to the customer.

What languages does it speak?

Our Voice Agents are fluent in over 50 languages including English, Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, and Spanish. It can even auto-detect the caller's language and switch instantly.
